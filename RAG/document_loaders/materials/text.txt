A mountain high, a distant goal,
Is climbed one step, one heart, one soul.
No leap can take you to the peak,
It favors those who strive each week.

The seed beneath the soil below
Must patiently endure and grow.
Through wind and rain, through heat and cold,
Its silent effort turns to gold.

When failures come and hopes seem thin,
Hard work begins its task again.
For every stumble on the way
Becomes a lesson for the day.

Success is not a sudden spark,
But countless hours spent in the dark.
A masterpiece, a dream come true,
Is built by all the work you do.

So keep your purpose strong and clear,
Move forward, even through your fear.
For those who labor, learn, and strive,
Will see their deepest dreams arrive.
